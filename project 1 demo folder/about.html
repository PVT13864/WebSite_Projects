It was the vision of Raizada (late) Dr. Kedar Nath Modi that transformed into the noble inception and unsurpassed success of Dr. K. N. Modi Foundation, a reputed and respected educational conglomerate that encompasses various individual educational entities. The foundation has made immense contributions toward society and the nation by establishing contemporary professional and technical education facilities at every level.

Our Values

Dr. K. N. Modi Foundation is built on the pillars of Quality Education, Social Service, Positive Attitude, Commitment, Dedication, and Promoting Indian Values. The foundation not only focuses on setting up professional and technical institutes but also embraces a progressive approach by establishing a Sanskrit Vidyapeeth with degrees in Acharya and Shastri, where teaching follows the Gurukul Methodology.

Our Heritage

With a heritage of over seventy years, Dr. K. N. Modi Foundation continues to advance on a path of excellence, providing quality education at multiple levels. Our institutions are equipped with world-class infrastructure and facilities to ensure effective learning experiences.

Eminent Faculty

The foundation has appointed distinguished faculty members across its institutions who not only excel in their academic fields but also focus on holistic education and character building based on values and principles.

Location

KNMIET is located at Modinagar on National Highway-58, approximately 32 km from the Delhi border.
